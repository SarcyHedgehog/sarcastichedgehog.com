Vibe-Coded Multiplayer Prototypes

These apps are built using Vibe Coding — prototyping in real-time with AI and the Multisynq multiplayer engine. They're experimental, open-source, and yours to fork or improve.

Start a game, then share the URL with a friend — or scan the QR code shown inside the game.

VoteTogether

Multiplayer voting and prediction tool. Vote on polls and guess what the majority will choose. Perfect for team sessions and daily polls.

Features: Persistent host role, session-based rooms, scoring system, copy links & QR codes.

MazeTag

Multiplayer maze escape with traps, dashes, and close calls. Great for quick sessions with friends.

Controls: Use Left and Right Arrow keys to turn. Avoid the hunter!

BreakPong 3D

Pong meets Breakout in 3D — bricks, shrinking paddles, portals, and frantic back-and-forth multiplayer.

Controls: Arrow keys to move your paddle. Score by getting the ball past your opponent.

DemoDerby

Multiplayer demolition derby prototype. Smash opponents, dodge chaos, and test your driving instincts.

A multiplayer true/false quiz scorer. Join a session, hit the buzzer, and race to answer. Installable as a Progressive Web App.
